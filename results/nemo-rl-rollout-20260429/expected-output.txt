Warm-state D+C p50 settled to approximately 740us on 2-node p5en
against the deepep-v2-integration baseline. Wider range (600us-900us)
is acceptable; what matters for this PR is the presence of the
`NCCL INFO NET/OFI Using Amazon EFA` line. Without the Dockerfile
fix, you get `NCCL INFO NET/Plugin: Using network Socket` instead
and D+C p50 grows into the tens of milliseconds.
